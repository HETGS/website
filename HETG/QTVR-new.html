VIRTUAL REALITY TOURS OF THE HETG FACILITIES LOCATED AT MIT


Take a Virtual Reality Tour of the HETG Test/Alignment Facility or the Space Microstructures Laboratory using QuickTime Virtual Reality (QTVR) produced by Apple Computer. QuickTime can be downloaded for free from Apple by clicking here. You must have QuickTime in order to view these virtual reality tours. You can pan around by clicking your mouse and moving it in the direction that you want to go and zooming in/out is done with the option and control keys.
